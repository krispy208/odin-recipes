Cheese Burger

Description

This Cheese Burger Recipe is made with american cheese, beef, and a creamy tang of buffalo sauce.

Ingredients

  • Sesame Bun
  • American Cheese
  • 100% Beef
  • Spicy Buffalo Sauce

Steps

  1. Grill the patty
  2. Add Cheese
  3. Add the buns
  4. Add Buffalo Sauce
